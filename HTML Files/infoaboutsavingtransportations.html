A guide on how to perceive student/teacher requests about the transportation


The Epoka has updated its system by putting the booking the teacher/student has to make about choosing his preferred transportation method. You need to go to the left of your page and two sections, "Student Transport Information" and "Teacher Transport Information" will appear.

You can choose one of them, if you want to view the status of the bookings for either the student or the teacher. Anything saved by them will be sent to this page. Meanwhile, you can watch two videos on advisor tips.
